About Me

Hello! My name is Cintya, and I am passionate about web development and design. I have always been interested in the art of crafting visually appealing and functional layouts, and I take great pleasure in utilizing my skills to design aesthetic and responsive websites. My journey in web development began many years ago, when I first created a myspace page. Since then, I have been on a continuous learning journey, exploring the latest trends and technologies to stay at the forefront of web development. Aside from my professional life, I enjoy knitting and biking.When I'm not coding, you can find me tending to my garden or knitting something cozy. I am committed to excellence, continuous improvement, and delivering outstanding results. I am always eager to take on new challenges and create meaningful experiences. Thank you for visiting my portfolio, and I look forward to connecting with you!
